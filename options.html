Import History from CSV

Stream and archive external browsing history locally. Works with CSV files from Google Takeout or other history exporters.

Required CSV Columns:

  • url - The visited URL (e.g. https://google.com)
  • title - Page title (e.g. Google)
  • timestamp - Visit epoch milliseconds or seconds (e.g. 1716723223000)
